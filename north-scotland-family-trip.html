Saturday evening in Nairn arrive ~5–6 PM

Check in, drop bags, then a free long-daylight evening (sunset ~9:45 PM):

  • Nairn Central Beach & promenade — Marine Rd, Nairn IV12 4EA. Wide flat sand, play area, toilets, ice cream; bottlenose dolphins sometimes offshore. Free parking. FREE
  • Nairn Harbour — Harbour St, Nairn IV12 4PH. Pretty boats, short stroll, crab-watching. FREE
  • Dinner in town (chip shops, pubs, Indian/Italian on the High St) then back for an early night before the big Sunday.

Toilet & kid logistics 3 adults + 2 kids, one car

  • Toilets on tap: Luss, Glencoe Centre, Glenfinnan, Fort William, Fort Augustus, Drumnadrochit (Sat); Loch Morlich, Folk Museum, House of Bruar, Pitlochry, Hermitage (Sun).
  • The one stop with NO toilets: Ruthven Barracks — go at the Highland Folk Museum 5 min before. (Clava Cairns now pairs with Culloden's NTS centre, which has toilets.)
  • No real walking anywhere — Glenfinnan uses the flat under-the-arches path (skip the 20-min hill); everything else is roadside or a short flat stroll.
  • Fuel discipline: the A82 is remote — top up in Fort William before Loch Ness. A9 home has plenty (Aviemore, Pitlochry).
  • Pack a picnic + flasks; cap each stop to the times shown to keep momentum.
