Saturday evening in Nairn arrive ~8:45 PM

After the big Skye day, check in (reception open till 10 PM), drop bags, then catch the last of the long daylight (sunset ~9:45 PM):

  • Nairn Central Beach & promenade — Marine Rd, Nairn IV12 4EA. Wide flat sand, play area, toilets, ice cream; bottlenose dolphins sometimes offshore. Free parking. FREE
  • Nairn Harbour — Harbour St, Nairn IV12 4PH. Pretty boats, short stroll, crab-watching. FREE
  • Dinner in town (chip shops, pubs, Indian/Italian on the High St) then back for an early night before the big Sunday.

Toilet & kid logistics 3 adults + 2 kids, one car

  • Toilets on tap: Glencoe Centre, Fort William, Eilean Donan, Sligachan, Portree (Sat); Loch Morlich, Folk Museum, House of Bruar, Pitlochry, Hermitage (Sun).
  • The one stop with NO toilets: Ruthven Barracks — go at the Highland Folk Museum 5 min before. (Clava Cairns/Culloden has the NTS centre car-park toilets.)
  • No real walking anywhere — everything is roadside or a short flat stroll; Portree gives the kids a proper run-around and ice cream.
  • Fuel discipline: the A82/A87 out to Skye is remote — fill up in Fort William, the last reliable fuel before the island. A9 home has plenty (Aviemore, Pitlochry).
  • Pack a picnic + flasks; cap each stop to the times shown to keep momentum.
