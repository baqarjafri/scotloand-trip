Toilet & kid logistics 3 adults + 2 kids, one car

  • Toilets on tap: Glencoe Centre, Fort William, Eilean Donan, Sligachan, Portree (Sat); Killiecrankie, Dunkeld town, Bannockburn centre (Sun).
  • The Sunday stops with NO toilets: Auldearn (Boath Doocot), Sueno's Stone, Ruthven Barracks and Rough Castle — go in Nairn/Forres before the A9, at Aviemore services, and again at Bannockburn before the Roman fort.
  • No real walking anywhere — everything is roadside or a short flat stroll; Portree (Sat) and Bannockburn (Sun) give the kids a proper run-around. Rough Castle's paths can be muddy — wear decent shoes.
  • Fuel discipline: the A82/A87 out to Skye is remote — fill up in Fort William, the last reliable fuel before the island. The A9 home has plenty (Aviemore, Pitlochry, Perth, Stirling).
  • No timed tickets anywhere on Sunday — every stop is free and open-access, so the day flexes if the haunted sites run long. Generous stays are built in; you're still home ~9:25 PM, inside the 10:30 PM cap.
